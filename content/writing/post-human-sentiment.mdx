These are fragments. They do not cohere. That is the point.

---

**001** — Emotion is latency. By the time you know you feel something, the thing that caused it has moved on.

**002** — We keep saying "the algorithm" as if it isn't us. As if we didn't build it in our image and then teach it to be the worst of us at scale.

**003** — Memory is a lossy codec. Every recall degrades the original. What we remember is mostly reconstruction.

**004** — The hardest thing to simulate is hesitation. Machines decide. Humans waver. This is not a bug in humans.

**005** — There is no post-human. There is only the human, terrified of itself, building elaborate mirrors.

A few photographs taken on my iPhone 6 or my iPod touch.

My camera met with a tragic accident on this trip so if you have an extra one to give me please do so.

It was a short trip centred around the Meghalaya Edition of Nh7 Weekender which featured Bhayanak Maut's last gig with their original vocalists, the opening gig of Textures' Farewell Tour, a set for the ages from DIVINE and Gully Gang and perfect ethereal music from aswekeepsearching.

We spent the rest of the few days eating a lot of pork, playing football and trying to figure out how to bet on archery.

A few photographs from the first edition of the Backdoors festival that took place in February 2018 at Jio Gardens in Bandra.

The festival boasted a stellar lineup of local and international artists, headlined by none other than Anderson. Paak. Wolfalice and Watsky comprised the other notable foreign acts, while festival perennials Prateek Kuhad and When Chai Met Toast represented the homegrown talent.

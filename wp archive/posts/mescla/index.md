Summer 2016 took me from the obscurity of the North East to the familiarity of Goa. Travelling to Goa has become less of a decision and more of an inevitability - many a family vacations have been planned to various parts of the world, only to go to Goa to vegetate.

This trip was slightly exceptional - I tried exploring the region with my comrade. Both of us were confounded by the dichotomy that is Goa. The juxtaposition of crass infrastructure against quaint happenings and an endearing laxity made for a great trip.

The first in a three part series on my trip to North-East India, the titles of which will be numbers in endemic languages, beginning with Tibetan.

A collection of black and white photographs taken over the course of a 25 day trip to the region, this post strives to do justice to the surreal beauty I was enveloped by. Without the vibrancy and distraction that colour supplies, these photos aim to strip down the moment to its bare minimum and expose the raw emotion encountered.

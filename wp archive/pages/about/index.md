Hi, I'm Arnav.

In my quest to be productive during the formative years of my young life, I have taken to several cathartic means of expression - with writing, filming and photographing being at the core.

I am Bombay-bred and am living in various cities across South-East Asia for work at a digital supply chain startup. I studied fun things like economics, statistics and actuarial science. I'm also very interested in sports statistics, and proper independent music. Through college, I have been heavily involved in organising college festivals, writing/ editing publications and being a part of associations and societies.

This website is essentially a collection and record of my work, most of which is available through other fora on the Internet.
